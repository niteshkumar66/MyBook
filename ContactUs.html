Privacy & Security

At "MyBook", we value your trust and are committed to protecting your privacy and securing your personal information. This Privacy & Security Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

When you use MyBook, we may collect the following types of information:

Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, shipping/billing address, and payment details.

Account Information: Username, password, and order history.

Browsing Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and usage data collected via cookies and similar technologies

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

Process and fulfill your orders

Communicate with you regarding purchases, updates, or support

Personalize your shopping experience

Improve our website and services

Send promotional offers (only if you opt in)

3. Data Security

We implement a variety of industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information, including:

SSL encryption for secure transactions

Secure payment gateways

Regular security audits and monitoring

Access control and authentication measures for sensitive data

4. Sharing of Information

We do "not sell" or rent your personal data. We may share information only with trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website or servicing you, such as:

Payment processors

Shipping partners

Email service providers

All such partners are required to maintain the confidentiality and security of your information.

5. Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to:

Access and update your personal information

Opt out of marketing communications at any time

Request deletion of your account and data

To exercise these rights, contact us at Mail to MyBook

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your experience, analyze site usage, and provide personalized content. You can manage your cookie preferences in your browser settings.

7. Children's Privacy

MyBook does not knowingly collect information from individuals under the age of 13. If we become aware of such data, it will be deleted promptly.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy & Security Policy from time to time. We encourage you to review it periodically. Continued use of our website after changes means you accept those updates.
